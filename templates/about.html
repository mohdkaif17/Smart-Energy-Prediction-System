About SmartEnergy AI

SmartEnergy AI is a mini project that uses Machine Learning to predict household energy consumption based on environmental conditions and time features.

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • ML: Random Forest (scikit-learn)
  • Visualization: Chart.js

Built as an academic mini project.
